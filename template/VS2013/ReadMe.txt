This application generates a procedurally-generated city with a variety of buildings of different shapes and sizes.
Other features include a skybox and fog.

CONTROLS
========

	W     -	 Move forward
	S     -  Move backwards
	A     -  Translate left
	D	  -  Translate right
	Space -  Jump!

Use the mouse cursor to move the camera.
